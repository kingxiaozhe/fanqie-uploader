# 隐私权政策 / Privacy Policy

最后更新 / Last updated: 2026-06-14

## 中文

「番茄小说批量发布助手」（以下简称"本扩展"）尊重并保护您的隐私。本政策说明本扩展如何处理数据。

### 我们收集什么
**本扩展不收集、不上传、不分享任何个人数据。**

- 本扩展**没有任何后端服务器**，不会把任何数据发送到开发者或第三方。
- 您选择的本地章节文件（标题、正文）仅在您的浏览器内处理，用于填写到番茄小说作者后台。
- 发布设置、任务进度、运行日志仅保存在浏览器本地存储（`chrome.storage.local`）中，存在您自己的设备上，您可随时清除。

### 权限说明
- **storage**：在本地保存发布设置、任务进度与运行日志。
- **tabs**：打开/关闭番茄小说的章节发布标签页，以完成批量发布。
- **notifications**：在发布完成或失败时给出桌面通知。
- **sidePanel**：显示上传进度面板。
- **访问 fanqienovel.com**：仅在番茄小说作者后台页面内运行，读取/填写章节内容并完成发布；不在其他任何网站运行。

### 数据去向
所有数据**只停留在您的本地浏览器**，不经过任何网络传输到开发者侧。本扩展与番茄小说官方无关联。

### 联系方式
如有疑问，请通过本扩展的代码仓库提交 issue。

## English

"Fanqie Novel Batch Publisher" (the "Extension") respects your privacy. This policy explains how the Extension handles data.

### What we collect
**The Extension does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

- The Extension has **no backend server**; it never sends any data to the developer or any third party.
- Local chapter files you select (titles, body text) are processed only within your browser, solely to fill them into the Fanqie Novel author console.
- Publishing settings, task progress, and run logs are stored only in local browser storage (`chrome.storage.local`) on your own device, and can be cleared by you at any time.

### Permissions
- **storage**: save publishing settings, task progress, and run logs locally.
- **tabs**: open/close Fanqie Novel chapter-publishing tabs to perform batch publishing.
- **notifications**: desktop notifications when publishing finishes or fails.
- **sidePanel**: show the upload progress panel.
- **Access to fanqienovel.com**: runs only on the Fanqie Novel author console pages to read/fill chapter content and publish; it does not run on any other website.

### Data flow
All data **stays in your local browser** and is never transmitted to the developer. The Extension is not affiliated with Fanqie Novel.

### Contact
For questions, please open an issue in the Extension's code repository.
